Hiring Data Engineers

Data engineering is the backbone of modern analytics and AI capability. Without reliable pipelines and governance, insight and AI investment struggle to land.

What differentiates strong data engineers

  • Ownership of pipeline reliability and data quality
  • Cloud platform fluency
  • Understanding of cost optimisation
  • Collaboration with analytics and ML teams

Common hiring challenges

  • Blurring platform engineering and analytics engineering
  • Underestimating DevOps and CI/CD requirements
  • Hiring senior titles without defined scope

Our approach

We validate the maturity of your platform and clarify whether you need a builder, optimiser or architect. Search is then aligned to technical stack, growth plan and commercial outcomes.
