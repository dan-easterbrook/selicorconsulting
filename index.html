Why clarity before recruitment matters

Many organisations hire into AI and data because they feel they should. The title sounds right and the brief looks impressive, but underneath it is often unclear.

  • Ownership is unclear
  • Reporting lines are political
  • Data foundations are weak
  • Success criteria are vague

Before we go to market, we pressure test the structure:

  • What problem the role is truly solving
  • Whether it is strategic, operational or both
  • What authority the hire will actually hold
  • What good looks like in 12 months

It is light touch. One focused conversation, a clear written summary, and a sharper hiring plan. Then we execute the search with confidence.

Our approach

  • 1. Capability validation – a structured conversation to define role purpose, ownership, success metrics and hiring sequence.
  • 2. Retained and exclusive search – focused search aligned to validated structure, not a vague job title.
  • 3. Market and talent mapping – clear visibility of available capability, compensation realities and where the trade-offs sit.
  • 4. Long term hiring strategy – ongoing support as AI capability evolves, so hiring stays aligned to outcomes.
