About Selicor Consulting

I help organisations build high-performing data, analytics and AI teams — without the noise, the vague job specs or the six-month hiring drift.

With more than 18 years recruiting across data, analytics, data science, engineering and AI, I run Selicor Consulting in partnership with Scot Lewis Associates — a UK-based search firm trusted by leaders who want straight answers, thoughtful shortlists and hires that actually stick.

My approach is practical and outcomes-first: define the problem, shape the role around the outcomes, then map the market. I work closely with CDOs, Heads of Data, CTOs and CEOs across financial services, insurance, and a range of scale-ups and SMEs where speed and fit matter. I also partner with sports organisations on analytics and performance roles, bringing the same rigour without the hype.

What clients value is the mix of market depth and candour. I’ll tell you when a spec won’t land, when salary bands are off, and when an “AI” brief is really a data foundation brief in disguise. I keep things anonymous when needed, protect your brand in the market, and prioritise long-term fit over short-term activity.

How I Work

  • Consultative search, not keyword bingo – role shaping, market mapping, and targeted outreach to the right people, not everyone.
  • Clarity before candidates – success metrics, must-haves vs nice-to-haves, and what “great in 12 months” looks like.
  • Shortlists you can defend – evidence-based profiles, context from real conversations, and honest trade-offs.
  • Trusted and discreet – I represent both you and the candidate with care; anonymous adverts available where appropriate.
